## 🔍 Domain & Brand Considerations

### **Domain Availability Check**
Before finalizing, check availability for:
- `.com` (most important)
- `.app` (for mobile apps)
- `.finance` (industry-specific)
- `.io` (tech companies)

### **Trademark Search**
- Check USPTO database
- Search international trademarks
- Consider hiring a trademark attorney

### **Social Media Handles**
- Check availability on Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn
- Consider variations if exact name is taken

## 📝 Naming Criteria Used

### **✅ What Makes a Good Name:**
- **Memorable**: Easy to remember and spell
- **Relevant**: Related to finance or money management
- **Scalable**: Works as the app grows
- **Available**: Domain and trademark availability
- **Brandable**: Works for logos and marketing
- **Professional**: Appropriate for financial services

### **❌ What to Avoid:**
- **Generic terms**: "Finance", "Money", "Budget" (hard to trademark)
- **Complex spelling**: Hard to remember or type
- **Negative connotations**: "Loss", "Debt", "Risk"
- **Limited scope**: Too specific to one feature
- **Cultural issues**: Potential international problems
